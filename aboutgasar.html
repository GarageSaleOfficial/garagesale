About Gasar

Garage Sale, or G-Sale, is an app that will simplify the market for everyone. It allows users to take an image of a product they wish to sell, receive average prices for that type of product, and list it on the market for others to purchase—all with one app. All a user has to do is simply point their phone’s camera at an object and add some key words, and prices will be shown on their screen. Suggested prices shown are generated from an average of the highest and lowest prices of that specific product, and the condition of the product is taken into consideration as well.

Not only can users sell items, but they can also search and purchase items that other people are offering for sale. Users can input keywords and search possible targets by price or condition. In addition, users can keep of track of items that they have bought and sold.
